How It Works

  1. Commit to a behavior that you are going to change
    • For example, "Walking", "Drinking Water," or "Smoking"
  2. Decide on the units you will use to count your behavior
    • For example, "steps," "oz," or "cigarettes"
  3. Enter whether you want to increase or decrease your behavior
    • For example, "increase" walking or "decrease" cigarette smoking
  4. Write a strong reason for why you want and need to improve your behavior
    • This will stay posted at the top of the page to remind you if things get tough
  5. Track your baseline behavior and submit an entry for your total each day
    • Don't make any changes in the behavior, just do what you normally would do.
  6. After you have 3 baseline entries, you will be able to begin interventions
    • Your goal will be automatically generated based on your baseline average
  7. Track your intervention behavior and submit an entry for your total each day
    • Meet your goal every day for at least 7 days, and use the motivational quotes for inspiration!
  8. Track your progress on your account page and celebrate your hard work!
